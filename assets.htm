Sports Party

Active Arcade is a fresh take on how to get active while having fun, just by playing a few simple games. It puts you inside the game and uses your natural body movement as the game controller. Active Arcade is a new digital playground that you can play in anytime, anywhere, with anyone.

 
 

You’re the star.

Play games by using the most natural game controller, your body. Our advanced body motion detection is powered by proprietary computer vision that was proven in the pro sports world, applied in an entirely new way with Active Arcade. Now you’re the star of the game unlike anything you’ve played before.

 
 

Play together.

Active Arcade is a completely new way for friends and families to play and get active together. Just like playing a pick-up game on the playground, or the board games we play on family game night — Active Arcade is a social activity. Multi-player games can be played together in-person or online.

Any space is now your playground.

Active Arcade transforms your world into a digital playground. We use the camera on any iOS or Android device—no extra equipment, sensors or wearables required. Connect to your TV for an even more immersive experience. Games just need a little space for you to move around, so you can play anywhere.

Something for everyone.

Active Arcade has a wide variety of motion-based games that anyone can play. Games have different game play, style and depth, so there’s something for players of any age or level of activity. There are quick and fun reaction games, family-style party games, intense action games, adventure games, and even music rhythm games.
